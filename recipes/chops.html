Perfect Pork Chops

Pork chops are not respected the way they should be. This recipe will show you just how to treat a pork chop right so you can experience its amazing succulence.

Ingredients

  • 4 Pork loin chops
  • Kosher salt
  • Pepper, freshly cracked
  • Seasonings of choice
  • 3 tbsp neutral-tasting oil
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 bunch of sage
  • 5 cloves garlic, smashed
  • Juice of half a lemon

Steps

  1. Season all sides of the pork chops generously with salt and pepper. Sprinkle with more seasonings (if using)
  2. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium heat. Add pork chops. Sear for 3 minutes. Flip and reduce heat to medium. Cook for another 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add the butter, sage, and garlic to the pan. Continuously baste the pork chops by spooning the melted butter over them.
  4. Squeeze the lemon over the pork chops. Remove them to a plate and let rest, uncovered, for 5 minutes.
