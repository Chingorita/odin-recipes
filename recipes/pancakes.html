Ricotta Pancakes

Description

The fluffiest ricotta pancakes you'll ever eat.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tbsp neutral-tasting oil
  • 1 cup ricotta
  • Unsalted butter
  • Topping(s) of choice
