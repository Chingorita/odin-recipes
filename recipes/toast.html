Avocado and Egg Toast

Description

Avocado and egg toast recipe. Millenials rejoice!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices sourdough bread (or bread of choice)
  • Unsalted butter
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 very ripe tomato
  • 1 large avocado
  • Sichuan chili oil, for drizzling
  • 4 soft-boiled eggs
  • Flaky sea salt
  • Torn Thai basil
