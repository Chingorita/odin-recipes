Avocado and Egg Toast

Avocado Toast, now with eggs

Avocado and egg toast recipe. Millenials rejoice!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices sourdough bread (or bread of choice)
  • Unsalted butter
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 very ripe tomato
  • 1 large avocado
  • Sichuan chili oil, for drizzling
  • 4 soft-boiled eggs
  • Flaky sea salt
  • Torn Thai basil

Steps

  1. Toast the bread either in a pan with butter or in a toaster.
  2. Cut the garlic clove in half, and rub the cut sides all over each slice of toast. Cut tomato in half and repeat technique.
  3. Slice the avocado thinly and arrange in a shingled pattern on the toast. Drizze with the chili oil, top each slice with a soft-boiled egg, and finish with a generous pinch of salt and pepper and torn basil.
